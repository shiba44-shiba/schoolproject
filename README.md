# Acts of Kindness - A Game About Making the World Better

A fun, engaging web-based 2D pixel art game about fighting injustice and spreading kindness. Play for 15 minutes and see how much good you can do!

## 🎮 Gameplay

**Objective:** Complete quests, defeat injustice, and spread kindness throughout the world!

### Mechanics:
- **Move**: Use Arrow Keys to navigate the world
- **Attack**: Press SPACE to fire kindness projectiles at injustice
- **Help NPCs**: Walk up to NPCs (blue characters) to help them and complete quests
- **Collect Hearts**: Defeat injustice to collect hearts that heal and increase your kindness meter

### Game Features:
- ✨ Pixel art visual style with colorful characters and enemies
- 📋 Dynamic quest system - help different people in need
- ⚔️ Combat mechanics - defeat personified injustice and inequality
- 💕 Kindness meter - track the good you spread
- ⏱️ 15-minute gameplay sessions for quick, engaging play
- 🎯 Scoring system - quests, defeated injustice, and kindness all contribute to your score

## 🌍 Themes Inspired by the Pyramid of Love

The game embodies principles of mutual aid and fighting injustice:
- **Protection**: Defeat the enemies of safety and equality
- **Compassion**: Help NPCs in need by completing their quests
- **Community**: Spread kindness to build a better world
- **Justice**: Work towards safety and fairness for all

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Use Arrow Keys to move your character
3. Press SPACE to attack enemies
4. Walk into NPCs to complete quests
5. Collect dropped hearts to heal
6. Try to accomplish as much good as possible in 15 minutes!

## 📊 Scoring

- **Quest Completion**: +100 points per quest
- **Defeating Injustice**: +50 points per enemy defeated
- **Kindness Spread**: +1 point per kindness unit collected

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **Phaser 3**: Game framework for 2D games
- **Canvas/HTML5**: Web-based rendering
- **Pixel Art**: All graphics generated programmatically

## 💡 Game Design Philosophy

This game encourages players to think about:
- The importance of community support
- Standing up against systemic injustice
- The power of acts of kindness
- Working together for a safer and more equal world

The mechanics combine action (fighting injustice) with purpose (helping those in need), creating a meaningful gaming experience that goes beyond entertainment.

## 🎨 Pixel Art Style

All graphics are procedurally generated using Phaser's graphics rendering, creating a cohesive pixel art aesthetic:
- **Green Player**: You, spreading kindness
- **Red Enemies**: Personified injustice and inequality
- **Blue NPCs**: People in need of help
- **Pink Hearts**: Kindness and healing
